Treat Yourself to an Unforgettable Thai Experience

Indulge in a journey of renewal for body and mind. Our expert therapists artfully combine traditional Thai techniques with contemporary approaches, promoting holistic wellness. Discover our diverse range of treatments, from classic Thai massage to aromatherapy, deep tissue, hot stone, and more.

  • Head, Back and Shoulders Massage

    60 Minutes £55

    90 Minutes £80


    This focused treatment targets the head, neck, shoulders, and upper back areas. It's ideal for those experiencing stiffness or pain in these regions, particularly due to computer use or poor posture. The massage combines various techniques to release tension, improve circulation, and promote relaxation in the upper body. It's an excellent choice for quick relief from work-related stress and muscle tightness.

  • Reflexology Foot Massage

    60 Minutes £60

    90 Minutes £85


    This specialized treatment involves systematic pressure application to specific points on the feet and lower legs, sometimes using a wooden tool. It's excellent for foot aches and general well-being. Based on the principle that foot points correspond to body organs, this massage aims to release blockages and promote overall health. The stimulation of these points is believed to have beneficial effects throughout the body, making it a holistic treatment.

  • Hot Stone Massage

    60 Minutes £75

    90 Minutes £95


    In this deeply relaxing treatment, heated stones of various sizes are placed on and used to massage the body. The combination of the stones' hardness and penetrating heat creates a deep tissue effect, helping to soften and release long-held tensions. The warmth of the stones enhances muscle relaxation, improves circulation, and promotes a sense of calm and well-being. It's an excellent choice for those seeking both relaxation and therapeutic benefits.
